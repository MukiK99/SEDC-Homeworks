Delivery time

When you have received funding for a created order, the order enters processing status. It will then be processed and confirmed and sent for delivery. Delivery time is 3 working days. All Friday or weekend orders will be processed on the first following business day. The working hours of our online shop are from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. during the week and Saturday from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m.

Delivery collection

The courier service ExclusivePost (the company that does the delivery for us) delivers the parcels to the delivery address with the following working hours: ExclusivePost every working day from 8 am to 4 pm and Saturday from 9 am to 2 pm. We ask that during that period there be a person who can receive the delivery. When taking over the shipment, it is necessary to carry out a visual inspection of the package, in case there are no physically visible damages. If you notice any damage to the package itself or suspect that the product may be damaged, you should refuse the shipment and at the same time notify us by calling the phone number 073 112 223.If everything is fine with the shipment without any damage, feel free to pick it up and sign the delivery note that the courier will give you. It is a common practice that if the courier does not find you at the designated address, he will have to contact you on the contact phone number that you indicated when creating the order and make an agreement for a new delivery date for the order. If, even in that case, he does not find you again at the indicated address, the order is returned to us. After we receive it, we will contact you to come to a conclusion and arrange for resending.
